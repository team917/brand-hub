02 · The four buckets

Every task in your business goes in one of these.

You already sort tasks this way in your head. We're just naming it. Then we help you move things across.

Bucket 01

Runs itself

Routine work handled start to finish: invoices, follow-ups, reports, reminders. Set once, done every time.

Bucket 02

Checked by a person

Important work gets prepared for you and approved by a real person before it goes out. That person can be us, or someone from your own team.

Bucket 03

Stays with you

Judgment calls, relationships, the things only you can do. The AI stays out of it.

Bucket 04

Stops happening

Some work only exists out of habit. Let's find those too and switch them off. The cheapest task is the one nobody has to do anymore.
